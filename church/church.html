Food

We Service the unwanted and unhoused with love and dignity.

Disciples of Faith-The Hub is a Christian Fellowship that does ministry outside of the box by being a light in the community we serve. Our mission is to reach those that have been cast aways unhoused and abandoned by society and show them the love of Jesus Christ by fostering good and healthy relationships . We strive to not just feed clothe and house the unhoused but help them become self sufficient and learn lifeskills to last a lifetime through the word of God.
